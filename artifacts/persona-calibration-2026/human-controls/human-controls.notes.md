# Korean human controls

Collected 7 Korean pre-LLM-era (2018-2019) personal blog posts from Naver Blog PostView and Tistory.
Registers cover product review, exhibition opinion, fandom diary/visit recap, military-training advice, pet-product review, stationery fair diary, and restaurant review.
Selection favored explicit human markers: dated posts before 2021, idiosyncratic slang/typos/emoticons, first-person anecdotes, uneven paragraphing, and concrete personal context.
Raw text files are extracted body text only, with navigation/boilerplate removed where possible.
