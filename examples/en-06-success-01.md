# Pattern 6 (en): Formulaic "Challenges and Prospects" — Success Case

## Input Text

> Despite these challenges, the renewable energy sector remains poised for significant growth. While it remains to be seen how government policies will evolve, the industry stands at a crossroads. With continued innovation and collaboration among stakeholders, the road ahead looks promising. Only time will tell whether these obstacles can be overcome, but a bright future undoubtedly lies ahead.

## Expected Output

> The main bottleneck is grid storage. The U.S. currently has about 16 GW of battery capacity, but the DOE estimates 200 GW is needed by 2035 to support 80% renewable generation. Three utility-scale projects in Texas and Arizona are scheduled to come online in 2025, adding roughly 4.5 GW.

## Applied Pattern

- Pattern 6 (Formulaic "Challenges and Prospects"): The paragraph contains both generic challenge phrases ("despite these challenges", "whether these obstacles can be overcome") and generic optimism phrases ("poised for significant growth", "road ahead looks promising", "bright future lies ahead") — the classic two-step formula. Also includes "remains to be seen", "at a crossroads", and "only time will tell."

## Judgment

**Success** — The fire condition ("a paragraph or conclusion contains both a generic challenge phrase AND a generic optimism phrase") is clearly met. Six watch-word phrases appear in four sentences, forming the exact challenges-then-hope formula. No specific challenge is named, no timeline is given, and no concrete action is described. The corrected version identifies a specific bottleneck (grid storage), cites concrete figures (16 GW vs. 200 GW needed), names the authority (DOE), and lists actual projects with dates.
