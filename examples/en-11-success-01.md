# Pattern 11 (en): Elegant Variation (Synonym Cycling) — Success Case

## Input Text

> Seoul is one of the most densely populated cities in Asia. The South Korean capital is home to roughly 10 million residents. The bustling metropolis has expanded rapidly since the 1960s. This major East Asian urban center now accounts for nearly half of South Korea's GDP.

## Expected Output

> Seoul has about 10 million residents, making it one of the most densely populated cities in Asia. The city has expanded rapidly since the 1960s and now accounts for nearly half of South Korea's GDP.

## Applied Pattern

- Pattern 11 (Elegant Variation): Seoul is referred to by four different names in four sentences: "Seoul", "the South Korean capital", "the bustling metropolis", "this major East Asian urban center." Each is a synonym rotation to avoid repeating the city's name.

## Judgment

**Success** — The fire condition ("the same entity referred to by 3+ different names or synonyms within a single paragraph") is met with four synonyms for Seoul. None of the alternatives adds information — they are decorative substitutions. "The bustling metropolis" and "this major East Asian urban center" are particularly conspicuous. The corrected version uses "Seoul" and the simple pronoun-like "the city," which is how human writers handle repetition.
