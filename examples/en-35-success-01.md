# Pattern 35: Throat-Clearing Openers — Success

## Input Text

> Here's the thing: our churn problem isn't about pricing. Let me be clear — users leave in week one, before they ever see an invoice. The uncomfortable truth is that onboarding, not billing, drives cancellations.

## Expected Output

> Our churn problem isn't about pricing. Users leave in week one, before they ever see an invoice. Onboarding, not billing, drives cancellations.

## Applied Pattern

- Pattern 35 (Throat-Clearing Openers): Three throat-clearing openers in one paragraph — "Here's the thing:", "Let me be clear —", and "The uncomfortable truth is that." Each stages the following clause as candid while adding nothing to it.

## Judgment

**Success** — The fire condition is met: 3 openers far exceeds the 2+ threshold, and in every case the clause after the opener is the actual point. Deleting the openers loses no content; the claims land faster and read as more, not less, direct.
